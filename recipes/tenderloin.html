Marinated Grilled Pork Tenderloin

Description

This marinated pork loin cooks up nicely on the grill. It tastes best when marinated for 24 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 table spoon garlic
  • 500g pork tenderloin

Steps

  1. Marinate all ingredients together on a plate
  2. Preheat grill on medium heat and oil the grate
  3. Cook tenderloin for about 30 mins
