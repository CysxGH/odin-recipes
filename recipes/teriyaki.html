Salisbury Steak

Description

Grilled shrimps are nice, Teriyaki sauce is delicious. Try adding both together!

Ingredients

  • 5pc jumbo shrimps
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 1 cup honey

Steps

  1. Combine water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger in a small saucepan
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate
  3. Arrange skewers on the hot grate. Grill 2 to 3 minutes per side
